About
My Profile
I embarked upon my professional journey in product and service development with two FoodTech startups. My responsibilities revolved around deploying methods and tools to expedite product development timelines. I facilitated this by introducing rational design computational models and agile development methodologies. Being passionate about research and development, I always strive to combine specialised technical aspects with product development. Whilst acting as head of The Brunch Company's innovation department, we introduced a traceability system that motivated me to investigate the potential of blockchain technology.

The swift progressions within this ecosystem left a memorable impression on me and ignited my curiosity towards developing business solutions utilising this technology. I strongly believe the potential for the blockchain ecosystem to transform much of our current understanding is incalculable. As an enthusiast of innovation, I am eager to be a part of this field. Hence, I utilised my background in mathematical modelling of complex systems and shifted towards technical development of web3. My primary focus is to improve my hard skills while retaining my business perspective gained from my prior experience in innovation. My goal is to leverage my expertise in project management and business solution development to enhance the dynamic nature of innovation processes.
